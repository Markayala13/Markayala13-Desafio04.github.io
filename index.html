Knights of the Zodiac

The Gold Saints are the most powerful warriors serving the goddess Athena. They represent the twelve zodiac constellations and wear golden armor known as Gold Cloths, which provide them with immense protection and amplify their already extraordinary abilities. Each Gold Saint masters the energy of the universe, known as Cosmos, and can manipulate it to perform devastating attacks. Gold Saints are guardians of the Twelve Temples of the Zodiac, located on the path leading to Athena's sanctuary. Their speed is unmatched, as they can move at the speed of light, and they possess incredible knowledge of their respective zodiac sign and its powers.

Aries – Mu

Mu is the Gold Saint of Aries, known for his wisdom and calm demeanor. He’s a master in repairing Cloths and excels in telekinesis and defensive techniques like Crystal Wall.

Taurus – Aldebaran

Aldebaran is the Gold Saint of Taurus, a towering and powerful warrior. He values strength and honor, using his iconic attack, the Great Horn, to crush his enemies.

Gemini – Saga/Kanon

The Gemini Gold Saint has a dual personality, often represented by the twins Saga and Kanon. They possess incredible cosmic power and are feared for their Galaxian Explosion.

Cancer – Deathmask

Deathmask is the Gold Saint of Cancer, known for his cruelty and obsession with death. His attack, Sekishiki Meikaiha, sends his enemies' souls to the underworld.

Leo – Aiolia

Aiolia is the Gold Saint of Leo, a brave and noble fighter. He’s known for his speed and the powerful Lightning Plasma attack, which unleashes hundreds of light-speed punches.

Virgo – Shaka

Shaka is the Gold Saint of Virgo and is considered the closest to the gods. He meditates often and uses his divine attacks like Tenbu Hōrin to overwhelm his opponents spiritually and physically.

Libra – Dohko

Dohko is the Gold Saint of Libra and a wise, elderly warrior. He guards the legendary weapons of Libra and uses powerful techniques like Rozan Hyakuryuha (Hundred Dragons of Rozan).

Scorpio – Milo

Milo is the Gold Saint of Scorpio, known for his loyalty and precision. His signature move, Scarlet Needle, delivers excruciating stings to his opponents.

Sagittarius – Aiolos

Aiolos is the Gold Saint of Sagittarius, remembered for his loyalty to Athena and heroic sacrifice. He wields the Golden Bow and Arrow, capable of immense destruction.

Capricorn – Shura

Shura is the Gold Saint of Capricorn, renowned for his mastery of the Excalibur, a technique that turns his arm into a sword capable of cutting through anything.

Aquarius – Camus

Camus is the Gold Saint of Aquarius and a master of freezing techniques. He uses attacks like Aurora Execution to control ice and freeze enemies to absolute zero.

Pisces – Aphrodite

Aphrodite is the Gold Saint of Pisces, known for his beauty and deadly roses. His techniques, like Royal Demon Rose, combine elegance and lethal poison.
